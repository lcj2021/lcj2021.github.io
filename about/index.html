Chengjun (Channing) Liu (Lau)

Chengjun Liu (Channing) is currently a first-year M.S. student in Statistics at Fudan University.
His current research revolves the field of Information Retrieval and Retrieval-Augmentated Generation for Large Language Models.
His interests lie in exploring the synergy between symbolic and neural retrieval techniques, aiming to enhance the efficiency of high-precision retrieval.

Chengjun is also interested in Recommendation Systems and is currently interning at Tiktok-Rec-Core.
His objective is to combine the knowledge from IR and RecSys, aming to create innovative solutions that integrate academic research into practical production environments.
